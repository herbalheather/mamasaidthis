‚ùì Real Questions. Real Answers.

For when you're struggling and need to know you're not alone.

I'm exhausted all the time. Is this normal? ‚ñº
Yes. This is SO normal. It's not weakness‚Äîit's biology + responsibility + sleep deprivation. Try: 1) 15 min uninterrupted rest, 2) Move your body, 3) Drink water, 4) Tell someone. If it persists, call 211 or 988. You deserve support.
I feel like I'm failing. Everything feels impossible. ‚ñº
Your brain is lying to you. Depression, overwhelm, anxiety make everything feel broken. You're not broken. Try Mama Missions‚Äîcomplete ONE thing. That's a win. Then call 988 or text 741741. You're worth it.
How do I afford everything my kids need? ‚ñº
1) Check SNAP/WIC with our Benefits Calculator, 2) Call 211 for emergency help, 3) Find local food banks, 4) Use our Budget Tracker to find hidden savings, 5) Reach out‚Äîyou're not meant to do this alone.
I don't have time for myself. ‚ñº
This is unsustainable. Even 5 minutes matters. Try: 1) One Mama Mission just for YOU, 2) Wake 15 min early for quiet, 3) Ask for help (partner, friend, family), 4) Lower standards everywhere else. Self-care = survival.
My kids are struggling in school. What now? ‚ñº
1) Custom worksheets with our generator, 2) Try PhET or Desmos for hands-on learning, 3) Coding games build problem-solving, 4) Talk to their teacher, 5) Remember: your love matters more than grades.
