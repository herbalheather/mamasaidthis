About Heather

Hey, I'm Heather—a mom who's been through the fire and come out figuring it out, one chaotic day at a time. At 13 weeks pregnant with my first child, my world shattered when my mom passed suddenly—one night she was making dinner, the next she was gone. That positive test had already shifted everything, but this? It flipped my life upside down. I'd always helped care for my grandma, but now I stepped up as the main caregiver, all while navigating the unknown.

There were dark days when I wasn't sure how my kids would eat—I'd be first in line at the food pantry, heart pounding with quiet determination. Growing up, I never realized how good we had it. A year later, I welcomed a baby boy, my second, without ever having held an infant or babysat before. Then, in the thick of 2019's COVID chaos, as my oldest started kindergarten amid lockdowns, I gave birth to twin girls. No car, tight finances, zero clue what I was doing? I figured it out anyway.

Now, with four little ones under my wing, I build these free tools so you don't have to call every pantry, scramble in embarrassment, or feel alone in the mess. Mama said sharing's free—and that's the truth. Let's ease the chaos together. 🌻
